# Tastevo — Digital Restaurant Menu.

A beautiful, always-up-to-date digital menu that restaurants can share with a tap, scan, or link.

## What it is

Tastevo replaces the printed menu, the PDF stuck to a laminated card, and the out-of-date website page with a single living menu that diners can open on their own phones. Restaurants update dishes, prices, and availability in one place; guests see the changes instantly.

The menu is designed to look as appetizing as the food it represents — with photography, descriptions, dietary tags, and pricing presented in a clean, readable layout that works equally well on a phone at the table or a laptop across town.

## Who it's for

- **Independent restaurants and cafés** that want a professional online menu without hiring a developer.
- **Multi-location brands** that need consistent menus across every storefront.
- **Cloud kitchens and delivery-first concepts** where the menu is the entire storefront.
- **Pop-ups, food trucks, and seasonal menus** that change often and can't afford to reprint every week.
- **Diners with dietary needs** who want clear allergen, vegetarian, or vegan labeling before they order.

## Use cases

- Replace QR-code-to-PDF menus with something modern and mobile-first.
- Let diners browse the full menu before they arrive — reducing decision time at the table.
- Highlight specials, seasonal items, or today-only dishes without reprinting anything.
- Offer a single shareable link for social media, Google Business profiles, and reservation platforms.
- Communicate the brand's personality through design, photography, and tone of voice.

## Core capabilities

- **Organized menu browsing** grouped by course, cuisine, or category.
- **Rich item detail** — photos, descriptions, pricing, and tags for dietary preferences.
- **Informational pages** (About, FAQ, Contact, Resources) that complete the restaurant's story.
- **Contact flow** for reservations, inquiries, and feedback.
- **Responsive, appetite-inducing design** that reads cleanly on every screen.

## The idea in one line

Tastevo is the simplest way for a restaurant to put its menu online and keep it looking delicious — forever.
